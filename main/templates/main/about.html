About us

Synaptic is a newly started modern addiction rehab center that offers in-clinic treatment in Greece. It is a colloboration between psychiatrists from Sweden and Greece with decades of experience between them. We are inspired by the massive scientific insights that have emerged since the 1970’s into the complexities of addictive disorder. Evidence shows that addiction is a multifactorial condition, intimately linked to psychiatric and social factors in a manner that makes successful treatment impossible unless the patient’s entire life situation is taken into consideration. Addictive disorder is connected to certain substances’ potential to cause craving and physical problems for persons who are addicted, but also to a lack of social context and meaning in life. It is closely intertwined with several psychiatric and neuropsychiatric conditions that can obstruct recovery if not they, too, are treated. That is why Synaptic does not only offer traditional 12-step treatment, relapse prevention, and motivational therapy, but also continuously adapts the treatment options to the individual client.

We have access to experienced doctors, specializing in addiction medicine and psychiatry, seven days a week, as well as therapists and nurses trained in modern addiction care. Our approach is not to judge or moralize, instead we want to offer an alternative for those who face the greatest challenges in recovering from addiction, i.e. those who have a known or undiagnosed psychiatric comorbidity, those who have exhausted other options and tried different clinics but still relapse after completed treatment and those who have a weak or non-existent social network.

Synaptic closely monitors the clients’ wellbeing through all the various aspects that perpetuate the addiction, we can also offer treatment optimized for the individual patient. This may entail making a neuropsychiatric assessment at the clinic. It could also mean determining if a patient suffers from depression or anxiety disorder and requires psychotherapy or medication to treat these in order to maintain sobriety. We also offer the most up-to-date treatments for the addictive disorder itself, which is not always the case at addiction rehab clinics in Sweden. We follow research and developments in the field very closely and adjust our range of offerings accordingly.

Treatment of addictive disorders is currently going through a massive transformation, both in regard to psychotherapy and pharmacological options. In order to offer the very best, state-of-the -art treatment, Synaptic keeps a close tab on global research, so that clients have access to the specific treatment option for which there is most evidence for their particular problem. Addictive disorder is never a singular syndrome, rather it is a product of both the individual and the substance involved. How difficult it is to reach and maintain sobriety depends on which substance is being abused. The most addictive substances, which according to scientific evidence have the most negative effects on individuals and society, are opioids and alcohol. Synaptic is the only Swedish addiction rehab concept outside of the public health system that can offer a full range of addictive disorder treatments and, as opposed to public health facilities, our clients can receive such help continuously throughout their stay in Greece.

We are currently hosted within the GALENE clinic facilities. The clinic, which was founded in 1971, offers patients world class care for psyciatric disorders. You can read more about the clinic and see photos of the faclities at GALENE's website.
